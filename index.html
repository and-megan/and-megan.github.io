Here's a recent favorite riddle of mine:

There are 5 pirates on a ship with 100 gold dubloons. There's a captain, first mate, second mate, crewman, and cabin boy. The most senior pirate proposes a split and all of the other pirates vote aye or nay. If more than half of the pirates vote for the proposal, the coins will be distributed accordingly. If less than half of the pirates vote for the proposal, the pirate who made the suggestion is thrown overboard and the process begins again. The pirates are rational, good at math, greedy, untrusting, and would throw one another overboard if all other factors are equal. What proposal should the captain suggest?
